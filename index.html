Submit to OneDrive Owner publish page
  1. Open the OneDrive submission folder.
  2. Upload your .ply, .splat, .ksplat, or .spz file.
  3. Wait for the owner to review and approve the model.
  4. The owner publishes approved files to the hosted viewer.
  5. Refresh this dashboard, select the hosted model, then click Share.
